# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-03-25**

## Overview

claude-memorize is a Claude Code plugin that saves and replays operational step recipes locally on your machine. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Collected

This plugin does not collect any data. All information is stored exclusively on your local machine inside the plugin's installation directory:

- `skills/<name>/SKILL.md` — individual recipe skill files

Recipe files may contain command-line steps, placeholder names, and notes you choose to save. This content never leaves your machine.

## No Network Access

This plugin does not make any network requests. It reads and writes local files only.

## No Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with the author, Anthropic, or any third party.

## Claude AI Processing

When you use `/memorize:*` skills, the content of your conversation and recipe files is processed by Claude (via Claude Code) to extract and replay steps. This processing is subject to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).
